PyLearn

A Python workshop in the Summer

PyLearn was one of our most successful events ever, as well as one of the largest workshops ever held in MPSTME. Over the course of 1 week, we taught 100+ students how to code in Python!

GameScript

A simple Snake game using JavaScript

Being N.O.W.'s first event, GameScript has moved on to becoming the flagship event people expect from us. We taught people how to make the classic game of Snake in JavaScript - using just 100 lines of code!

Savishkar

Competing for the best Final Year Projects

Savishkar is an annual competition hosted by N.O.W. in collaboration with the Computer Engineering Department of our college. It is a place for Final Year Computer Engineering students to showcase their F.Y.P.s and compete for the best and most innovative ones.

Android App Competition

Shedding light on the most skilled App Developers

We collaborated with the Department of Computer Engineering to organize the Android App Competition in April 2018. With an audience of over 200 students, our skills were put to the test as this was the first major event that N.O.W. was a part of.

GitHack

A crash course on Git and GitHub

A quick and simple workshop on how people can contribute to Open Source projects on GitHub. This was in collaboration with Hacktoberfest, a month-long celebration of the Open Web culture with exciting prizes for participants.

3D Printing

A hands-on 3D Printing workshop

A small workshop where students were taught how 3D Printers work, as well as get a live, hands-on demo of the printers. And oh, free samples to take home!
